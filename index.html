Common Questions

What people
usually ask.

Is the quiz really free?
Yes. Eight questions, three minutes, and a real result — no email required, no catch. If it's useful you'll know it.
What's the difference between the quiz and the full assessment?
The quiz identifies your primary blind spot and gives you one action to take. The assessment goes deep — an AI conversation across multiple areas, a full report, and a 60-minute session with Mike to build your actual plan.
What happens in the 60-minute session?
Mike reviews your assessment report before the call. You spend the time walking through what came up, identifying one or two things to focus on, and building a specific action plan. You leave with something concrete.
Do I need to be tech-savvy for the AI tools focus?
Not at all. That's the point. The assessment meets you where you are and recommends tools that fit your actual situation — not a wishlist of software you'll never use.
What does ongoing support look like?
Weekly or biweekly sessions where Mike helps you actually implement what the plan called for — tool setup, process changes, accountability, and adjusting as you go. Priced based on scope.
How do I know this is worth my time?
Start with the free quiz. Three minutes. If the result doesn't feel like it's describing your actual situation, nothing lost. If it does — you'll want to go further.
